Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Fast Mailer?
Answer:

Fast Mailer is a free service that offers you a temporary email address. You can receive email from any other mail servers. After a day, your email address will be deleted.

How does Fast Mailer earn profit from doing this?
Answer:

A fun fact about Fast Mailer is that this service was first created just for fun. Our VPS is a Raspberry Pi 3B and we host this at home. Therefore, we don't charge you what we don't pay for.

How does Fast Mailer store my email?
Answer:

First, Fast Mailer fetches email from Postfix (email server). Then, we store a copy of your original email in a folder. This file will be review manually if there was any report. After that, we parse your email content from the original email file, and store that data into our database. After a period of time, your email data will be deleted from both database and folder.

How does Fast Mailer encrypt my email?
Answer:

We don't do that. Google takes responsibility of doing that.
